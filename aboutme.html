Hello there! I'm James Green, (like the colour). A 20 year old interactive design student, coffee extraordinaire and movie king. Currently living in the rainy, cold but still brilliant country Northern Ireland. My current home is in a small county, Down. My home town is surrounded by trees and mountains with beautiful scenery. I have a strong desire to travel the world and very a cliche dream of travelling and seeing the seven wonders of the world.

I love a fast paced environment. I am extremely competitive and always try my hardest in everything thing i do. I never want to end up hating what i do, i try my best to work in the field i prefer

3 words that describe me: Happy, collective and chilled.

My hobbies and sports I attend in life are: Music, gaming, sleeping and boxing. My favourite thing to do in the world is eat, I am obessed with food, I cook just for the sake of making food, often just handing the food out to siblings or friends.

I work part time as a waiter, this allows me to pester chefs and learn new skills, I secretly cook along with shows and then pretend it was my recipe the whole time.
